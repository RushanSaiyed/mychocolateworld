About

Inspired in countless ways, My Chocolate World caters to chocolate lovers with the chocolates from around the world. Having experienced the origins and composition of fine flavours, we craft excellence to serve the best chocolates to the customers.

Our obsession with quality extends to every step of the process using only high-quality ingredients. Being chocolate enthusiasts ourselves, we hope to serve an unparalleled chocolate experience, featuring the premium luxury chocolate brands alongside the aisle of crafted excellence in chocolates produced by us.
